# Grove Capital Funding

A modern, responsive website for Grove Capital Funding — connecting business owners with accessible funding solutions.

## Overview

Grove Capital Funding is committed to serving business owners of all sizes and stages. We specialize in delivering business funding and ensuring growth and success. Founded in 2024, we bridge the gap between businesses seeking capital and approval.

## Features

- **Responsive Design** — Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Image Carousel** — Interactive image gallery with navigation controls
- **Application Form** — Integrated lead capture form with JotForm backend
- **Service Listings** — Comprehensive overview of funding options
- **Contact Information** — Direct phone and email contact for team members
- **Accessibility** — Semantic HTML and WCAG-compliant design

## Funding Services

- Working Capital
- Same Day Funding
- Business Lines of Credit
- SBA Loans
- Revenue Based Approvals (no credit checks)
- Merchant Cash Advance
- Term Loans
- Equipment Financing
- Non-Collateralized Lending
- Funding from $10,000 - $5,000,000

## Tech Stack

- **HTML5** — Semantic markup
- **CSS3** — Responsive design with media queries
- **JavaScript** — Interactive carousel and form handling
- **JotForm** — Backend form processing
