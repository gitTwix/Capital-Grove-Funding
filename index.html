About

Grove Capital is committed to serving all business owners of all sizes and stages of their business. Here we specialize in delivering business funding to businesses and ensuring their growth and success. Our founder Chris Willems founded this company in 2024 when he realized there was a huge gap in businesses applying for funding and them actually being approved, he remembered a time where a previous business venture he had needed capital and was not able to secure it. Determined to help business owners nationwide, Grove Capital Funding was born.
